Company Overview

SBONGA TECHNICAL SERVICES

Sbonga Technical Services is a 100% black-owned company established in 2015 by Mr. Lwish Sifiso Zwane. Starting from a modest workshop, the company now stands as a leader in sheet metal fabrication and energy systems across South Africa. Mr. Zwane has over 13 years of experience in CNC programming and steel manufacturing.
